UNRIAVALED EXPERIENCE

The Lake Las Vegas Sports Club is the heart of the Lake Las Vegas community. Amenities include 24 hour fitness center, yoga studio, massage therapy room, skin care studio, member lounge and bar, golf practice greens and range, game room, pickleball courts, tennis courts, lap and lagoon pools, hot tub, steam rooms, and more!

EXPLORE THE CLUB

Contact us today to get details about membership opportunities, exclusive benefits, and explore this private amenity for the enjoyment of Lake Las Vegas® residents only. Lounge poolside and soak up the sunshine, bring the kids for water fun, play tennis on our pristine courts, or socialize and connect with your neighbors.
